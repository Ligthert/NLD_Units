The units are generally the same bit differ in terms of uniform.

The regular infantry come with the following cammo-types:
	WLD		Woodland
	DST		Desert
	WTR		Winter
	NFPT	NFP-Tan
	NFPG	NFP-Green
	JGL		Jungle
	UN_DST	UN Desert
	UN_WLD	UN Woodland
	80      80's

The special forces come in three types of cammo:
	SF		Black
	MTP		Korps Commando Troepen
	KM		Korps Mariniers

So you'll see this return in the units
